Ride vision technology

Using predictive vision to save riders’ lives

Ride Vision is a unique Advanced Rider Assistance System (ARAS) tailored to meet the safety needs of riders.

Our industry-leading Collision Aversion Technology (CAT™) is a fusion of Artificial Intelligence (neural networks)
and Computer Vision, and is designed to seamlessly integrate with all motorcycle and scooter makes and
models.

Ride Vision functions via the coordination between two wide-angle cameras, our patented Predictive Vision algorithm, a compact in-unit computer processor (ECU), and two unobtrusive mirror-mounted LED alerts (the operations of which are fully customizable within the Ride Vision app).

Using Ride Vision’s 360° wide-angle camera footage, our Predictive Vision algorithm analyzes the visual data within the system’s ECU to identify only critical threats to the rider. This selective alert system eliminates the need for expensive and cumbersome hardware.

Our CAT™ system is built to work with any make and model of two-wheeled vehicles, significantly reducing production cost and providing freedom of modularity to manufacturers.

Ride Vision customisable distance keeping alert

Ride Vision is hardwired directly to the vehicle’s battery, and draws less charge than a standard cellphone. The system’s two IP69K rated video units begin recording the moment the motorcycle is turned on, and feature 3-hour looped footage that is uploaded to the Ride Vision app.

Within the Ride Vision app, users can set custom features like Bluetooth audio pairing, alert cadence and frequency, and access system software updates.

Individual ride statistics and GPS tracking records are also stored within the Ride Vision app. Here, riders can access ride data like distance and locations traveled, lean angles, speed, and the number of alerts fired during each ride.

Users can access ride summaries within the app. Ride summary cumulative reports are broken down by weeks, months, and years — and include total distance, total alerts, and max speed data. These reports can be exported for personal use (fuel calculation and consumption estimates) company data, or even used for insurance reduction purposes.

Ride Vision’s affordable technology is the perfect combination of compact hardware and industry-leading collision-avoidance software. Our product is uniquely compatible with all motorcycle and scooter types, and is designed to save riders’ lives.
